Sports Infrastructure

United Sports Infrastructure

Transforming the landscape of sports in India

We are United Sports Infrastructure, a pioneer in developing, operating, and maintaining world-class sports facilities in India. Our vision is to revolutionize the sports industry in India by providing state-of-the-art infrastructure, technology, and services for athletes, coaches, fans, and communities. We believe that sports can inspire, empower, and unite people across the nation.


We have a team of experts with extensive experience and knowledge in various aspects of sports infrastructure such as architecture, engineering, management, marketing, and sustainability. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and expectations and deliver tailor-made solutions that meet their goals and budget. We also collaborate with local authorities, stakeholders, and partners to ensure the smooth execution and operation of our projects.


We have successfully completed over 50 projects in different sports domains such as cricket, hockey, football, basketball, badminton, Volleyball, Indoor Gym and swimming. Our projects have also created positive impacts on the society and the environment by generating employment opportunities, promoting sports culture, enhancing public health and safety, and reducing carbon emissions.


If you are looking for a trusted and experienced partner for your sports infrastructure project, look no further than United Sports Infrastructure. We are committed to delivering excellence and innovation in every aspect of our work. Contact us today for a free quote or visit our gallery to see some of our amazing projects.
