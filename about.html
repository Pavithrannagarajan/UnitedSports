ABOUT US

Welcome to United Sports Education and Coaching Centre in Erode, where excellence in sports education meets a passion for nurturing champions. With a rich legacy spanning 8 years, we have been a beacon of athletic development and character building, providing top-notch coaching in various sports disciplines. Our academy, founded by the visionary Chandrasekaran, embodies a commitment to our core values and a mission to empower aspiring athletes.

Our journey began under the visionary leadership of Mr. Chandrasekaran, a stalwart in the field of sports education. His dedication and expertise have been instrumental in shaping the academy's philosophy, focusing on holistic development and producing well-rounded athletes.

Dr. N. Chandrasekaran is a passionate sportsman who founded the first of its kind multi-sports coaching school in 2014. With a burning desire to be a sportspreneur, he left the sophisticated job of sports coordinator and director of physical education from international school, government school, and college and started the entrepreneurial journey with a vision to have an exclusive specialty multi-sporting residential school. He acquired undergraduate and master's degrees in physical education from the prestigious Annamalai University and a doctor of philosophy from Bharathiyar University. He pursued a postgraduate diploma in fitness as a value addition. He is a specialist trainer and coach in football, athletics, handball, basketball, volleyball, hockey, cricket, and softball. He is a state and national level player in football and cricket during school and college and made more representation with great achievements. He was instrumental and played an anchoring role in organizing and officiating the state level inter-university soccer, hockey, and athletic tournaments during the PG program in the years 2008-2009.

Key achievements

  1. Represented under 12, 14, 16, 18 state level 8 football championship tournaments.
  2. Won runner-up and second runner-up in football during school days at various state level republic day and Bharathiyar birth day celebration events.
  3. Participated in all India level inter-school football tournament in the year 2003.
  4. Represented in South India level inter-university football tournaments held at various universities.

Working achievements

  1. Worked as a team manager and team coach of Bharathiyar University, South India and inter-college tournaments, Coimbatore.
  2. Worked as an NCC officer at VLB Janakiammal College of Arts and Science, Coimbatore.
  3. Worked as a team coach of Coimbatore district football team.

Our Mission

  1. At United Sports, our vision is simple yet powerful.
  2. To create a world where every aspiring athlete has the opportunity to achieve their full potential.
  3. Creating minimum 10000 sustainable jobs by direct and indirect employment.
  4. We believe in nurturing talent and transforming it into excellence through education, dedication, and character building.

Our Vision

  1. Our Vision is to provide unparalleled sports education, coaching, and facilities that foster not only athletic prowess but also the development of individuals who exemplify sportsmanship, teamwork, and integrity. We aim to instill in our students the skills and values necessary for success, both on and off the field.

Our Values

  1. We strive for excellence in everything we do, from coaching techniques to facilities and support services.
  2. We uphold the highest ethical standards in our interactions, fostering an environment of trust and respect among our students, coaches, and staff.
  3. We are committed to providing access to sports education for all, regardless of background or ability, ensuring an inclusive and diverse community of athletes.
  4. We teach our students to face challenges with determination and resilience, building the mental strength needed to overcome obstacles both in sports and in life.
